Ora formalizziamo tali requisiti creando un dizionario di riferimento.

Dictionary

  • WasteService: distributed software system to manage and coordinate the separate collection of waste. That's the final product as a whole, to be delivered to customer.
  • Service area software:
  • Service area: a rectangular, flat surface, free from internal obstacles
  • Plastic Box: a container which contains objects made of plastic, with maximum capacity of MAXPB kg
  • Glass Box: a container which contains objects made of glass, with maximum capacity of MAXGB kg
  • Indoor: a location in the service area where the waste enter from
  • Transport trolley: a square-shaped DDR robot, with side length of RD, which is initially situated in its home location and performs a deposit action.
  • Home: a location in the upper right corner of the service area that indicates the starting point of the transport trolley
  • Deposit action: a macro action that describes the deposit of the waste-load in a container; it is divided in:
    • pick up a waste-load from a waste truck on the Indoor
    • go from Indoor to the proper container
    • deposit the waste-load in the container
  • waste-load: it indicates a load of waste taken from a waste track
  • TruckLoad: a variable that indicates the weight of waste deposited by a waste truck in Indoor
  • WasteServiceStatusGUI: a software product that shows the state of the transport trolley and where it is in the service area, hom much weight contains each container and the state of the Led
  • Service-manager: an human supervisor that controls the state of the service-area using a WasteServiceStatusGUI
  • Sonar: an alarm device, which measures a distance, and if this one is less than a prefixed value DLIMT, the transport trolley must be stopped.
  • Led: a warning device, which worka ccording to the following scheme: - the Led is off when the transport trolley is at HOME - the Led blinks while the transport trolley is moving - the Led is on when transport trolley is stopped
  • DLIMT: a prefixed distance used to trigger the alarm system that stops the transport trolley
