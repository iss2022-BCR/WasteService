Nouns

Term Definition
WasteService Distributed software system to manage and coordinate the separate collection of waste. That's the final product as a whole, to be delivered to the customer.
Service area Rectangular flat surface, free from internal obstacles.
INDOOR Location situated on the perimeter of the service area, where the waste enters from.
PlasticBox Container which contains waste made of plastic, and has a maximum capacity of MAXPB.
MAXPB The maximum capacity of PlasticBox.
GlassBox Container which contains waste made of glass, and has a maximum capacity of MAXGB.
MAXGB The maximum capacity of GlassBox.
DDR robot Differential Drive Robot (DDR), which band is confined inside the service area. It's
Transport trolley ¹ Square-shaped virtual robot, with side length of RD, which is initially situated in its HOME location and performs a deposit action. It can move forward, backward, stop and rotate by 90°.
The transport trolley is a command actuator: it can use the DDR robot to perform actions in a real environment.
RD Length of each side of the transport trolley.
HOME Starting position of the transport trolley, inside the service area.
Deposit action Operation that describes the deposit of the WasteLoad in a container and consists in 3 phases:
  1. pick up a WasteLoad from a waste truck on the INDOOR;
  2. go from the INDOOR to the proper waste container
  3. deposit the WasteLoad in the container
Waste truck A generic entity external to the system, which deposits TruckLoad kg of waste at the INDOOR location.
TruckLoad The amount (kg) of waste that had been deposited by the waste truck at INDOOR.
WasteLoad The amount (kg) of waste the transport trolley has to pick up from the waste truck at INDOOR.
Service-manager An human supervisor which monitors the state of the service area through a WasteServiceStatusGUI.
State of the service area Information about the WasteService at a certain time, which include:
  • the current state of the transport trolley and its position inside the room;
  • the current weight (kg) of the material stored in the two waste-containers;
  • the current state of the Led.
WasteServiceStatusGUI Graphical User Interface of a specific software which shows the state of the service area and can be monitored by a supervisor.
Sonar Alarm device: when it measures a distance which is less than a prefixed value DLIMT, the transport trolley must be stopped.
It will be resumed when the Sonar detects again a distance higher than DLIMT.
Led Component used as a warning device:
  • OFF when the transport trolley is at HOME;
  • blinks when the transport trolley is moving;
  • ON when the transport trolley is stopped.
RaspberryPi Single-board computer. Refers to the model Pi 4 B.
DLIMT Prefixed distance used to trigger the alarm system that stops the transport trolley.

Notes:

  1. DDR robot and transport trolley are not the same.

Logical Architecture Model

  • WasteService: distributed software system to manage and coordinate the separate collection of waste. That's the final product as a whole, to be delivered to customer.
  • Service area software:
  • Service area: a rectangular, flat surface, free from internal obstacles
  • Plastic Box: a container which contains objects made of plastic, with maximum capacity of MAXPB kg
  • Glass Box: a container which contains objects made of glass, with maximum capacity of MAXGB kg
  • Indoor: a location in the service area where the waste enter from
  • Transport trolley: a square-shaped DDR robot, with side length of RD, which is initially situated in its home location and performs a deposit action.
  • Home: a location in the upper right corner of the service area that indicates the starting point of the transport trolley
  • Deposit action: a macro action that describes the deposit of the waste-load in a container; it is divided in:
    • pick up a waste-load from a waste truck on the Indoor
    • go from Indoor to the proper container
    • deposit the waste-load in the container
  • waste-load: it indicates a load of waste taken from a waste track
  • TruckLoad: a variable that indicates the weight of waste deposited by a waste truck in Indoor
  • WasteServiceStatusGUI: a software product that shows the state of the transport trolley and where it is in the service area, hom much weight contains each container and the state of the Led
  • Service-manager: an human supervisor that controls the state of the service-area using a WasteServiceStatusGUI
  • Sonar: an alarm device, which measures a distance, and if this one is less than a prefixed value DLIMT, the transport trolley must be stopped.
  • Led: a warning device, which worka ccording to the following scheme: - the Led is off when the transport trolley is at HOME - the Led blinks while the transport trolley is moving - the Led is on when transport trolley is stopped
  • DLIMT: a prefixed distance used to trigger the alarm system that stops the transport trolley
