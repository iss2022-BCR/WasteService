Sprint 0

During Sprint0 we will read the text provided by the client, analyzing the requirements that the final product needs to fulfill. Our task is to comprehend and formalize those requirements, so that everyone (team members and the customer/product owner) agrees on their meaning.

In particular, the requirement analysis aims to identify the actual requirements and the functionalities/features the product will need to include. Therefore, we are going to formalize the requirements specifically stated by the customer, or clarified through questions/interactions, which means they do not present ambiguities or alternatives of definition.

On the other hand, the problem analysis purpose is to express what we came up with, while trying to remove those ambiguities, and the reasonings and arguments that led us to a particular decision rather than another one.

As a consequence, the product of the analysis is going to be models (expressed in a particular modeling language), diagrams representing architectures and/or behaviours, and the related (executable) test plans. Then, from these, we will be able to draft some conclusions and define a work plan and the goal for the next Sprint: the Sprint1.
